Privacy Policy

We keep things simple. LobangKing.sg does not require an account to browse. Here's what we do and don't collect:

What stays on your device

  • Your theme choice (light or dark), your visit streak, and any deals you've upvoted are stored only in your browser's local storage. They never leave your device and we can't see them.

What we may collect

  • Email address — only if you choose to subscribe to our newsletter. We use it to send the daily deals email and nothing else. You can unsubscribe any time.
  • Deal submissions — details you send through the Submit form, including an optional email if you provide one.
  • Privacy-friendly analytics (optional) — if enabled, we use a cookieless service such as Cloudflare Web Analytics that reports only aggregate page views. It sets no cookies, stores no personal data, and never identifies you. We do not use Google Analytics or cross-site trackers.

What we never do

  • We don't sell your data. We don't share your email with advertisers. We don't track you across other websites.

Questions about your data? Reach us through the contact form.

Terms of Use

By using LobangKing.sg you agree to use the site for personal, lawful purposes. The deals, branding and content here are provided as a free community service. You may share links freely; please don't scrape, copy or republish our listings wholesale without permission.

Deal Disclaimer

We work hard to verify every promotion, but deals change fast. Prices, availability, codes and terms are set by the retailers — not by us — and can end or sell out without notice. Always confirm the final price and conditions with the merchant before purchasing.

Some outbound links may be affiliate links, meaning we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This never influences whether a deal is listed.
